About us

Providing personal training and group fitness since 2006.

Train with us in our fully equipped, custom-built gym in the heart of the City. We love coaching our clients in a supportive and motivating training environment, ensuring your training experience is rewarding and fulfilling.

We have a broad range of training options to suit every one, including CrossFit and Bootcamp, however personal training is our key service. At each session we focus on you! We evaluate your strengths & weaknesses and tailor training programs that meet your needs and help you reach your fitness goals.
